# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards
Respectful communication and collaboration are expected.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Violations may result in warnings or removal.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from Contributor Covenant.
